[Name]
Petre

[Oratio] P
O God, who didst give strength and learning to blessed Peter thy Confessor for the defence of the Catholic faith, mercifully grant, that by his example and teaching the erring may be saved and the faithful remain constant in the confession of truth. 
$Per Dominum

[Secreta] C 2
May the pious prayer of holy Peter thy Confessor and illustrious Doctor be not wanting to us, O Lord, but make our offerings acceptable to thee and ever win for us thy mercy. 
$Per Dominum.

[Postcommunio] C 2
May blessed N. thy Confessor and illustrious Doctor intercede for us, O Lord, that this thy sacrifice may obtain for us salvation.
$Per Dominum
