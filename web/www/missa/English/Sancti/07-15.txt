[Oratio]

kingdom from the throne of an earthly empire; we humbly beseech You, that as You~
enabled him, protected by the abundance of Your grace, to overcome the~
temptations of the world, so grant that we, in emulation of him, may shun the~
allurements of this world and come to You with pure hearts.
