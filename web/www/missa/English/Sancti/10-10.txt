[Oratio]
O Lord Jesus Christ, model of true humility and its reward, we beseech You, that~
as You made blessed Francis one of Your glorious imitators by his contempt for~
earthly honors, grant us to follow his example and to share in his glory.
$Qui vivis

[Secreta]
We beseech You, O Lord, may the holy Francis, prevail by his prayers, so that~
the offerings placed upon Your sacred altar may be beneficial for our salvation.
$Per Dominum

[Postcommunio]
May the pleading of the blessed Francis for us, together with the partaking of~
Your sacrament, protect us, O Lord, so that we, mindful of his way of life, may~
obtain the help of his intercession.
$Per Dominum
