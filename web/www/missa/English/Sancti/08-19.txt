[Oratio]
O God, Who wondrously enkindled St. John, Your Confessor, to promote the~
religious veneration of the Sacred Hearts of Jesus and Mary, and through him~
willed to found new religious families in the Church; grant, we beseech You,~
that we may honor his holy merits and may learn from the example of his virtues.

!Infra Octavam Assumptionis
O Lord, we beseech thee, forgive the transgressions of thy servants, and,~
forasmuch as by our own deeds we cannot please thee, may we find safety through~
the prayers of the Mother of thy Son and our Lord.
