[Oratio]
May the feast of the blessed Martyrs and Bishops, Cornelius and Cyprian, protect~
us, O Lord, we beseech You, and may their holy prayer recommend us to You.
$Per Dominum

[Commemoratio Oratio]
!For Sts. Euphemia, Lucy and Geminianus
O Lord, grant that our prayers may bear fruit in joy, that we who each year~
devoutly honor the death of the Martyrs, Euphemia, Lucy and Geminianus, may also~
follow them by steadfastness in faith.
$Per Dominum

[Commemoratio Secreta]
!For Sts. Euphemia, Lucy and Geminianus
Graciously look upon the prayers of Your people, O Lord, and grant that we may~
enjoy the spiritual help of those whose festival You permit us to celebrate.
$Per Dominum

[Commemoratio Postcommunio]
!For Sts. Euphemia, Lucy and Geminianus
O Lord, hear our prayers and comfort us by the continual assistance of Your~
Martyrs, Euphemia, Lucy and Geminianus, whose festival we solemnly celebrate.
$Per Dominum
