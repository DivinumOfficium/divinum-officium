90:1 He that dwelleth in the help of the Most High * shall abide in the shelter of the God of heaven.
90:2 He shall say unto the Lord: Thou art my helper and my refuge. * He is my God, and I will hope in Him.
90:3 For He shall deliver thee from the snare of the hunters * and from every troubling word.
90:4 With His shoulders will He overshadow thee, * and under His wings shalt thou have hope.
90:5 With a shield will His truth encompass thee; * thou shalt not be afraid for the terror by night,
90:6 nor for the arrow that flieth by day, † Nor for the thing that walketh in darkness, * nor for the mishap and demon of noonday.
90:7 A thousand shall fall at thy side, † and ten thousand at thy right hand, * but unto thee shall it not come nigh.
90:8 Only with thine eyes shalt thou behold, * and thou shalt see the reward of sinners.
90:9 For Thou, O Lord, art my hope. * Thou madest the Most High thy refuge;
90:10 No evils shall come nigh thee, * and no scourge shall draw nigh unto thy dwelling.
90:11 For He shall give His angels charge over thee, * to keep thee in all thy ways.
90:12 On their hands shall they bear thee up, * lest at any time thou dash thy foot against a stone.
90:13 Upon the asp and basilisk shalt thou tread, * and thou shalt trample upon the lion and dragon.
90:14 He shall cry unto Me, and I will hearken unto him. * I will shelter him because he hath known My name.
90:15 He shall cry to me, and I will hear him: ‡ I am with him in affliction, * and I will rescue him and glorify him.
90:16 With length of days will I satisfy him, * and I will show him My salvation.
