59:3 O God, Thou hast cast off and hast destroyed us; * Thou hast been wroth and hast had pity upon us.
59:4 Thou madest the earth to quake and troubled it; * heal the breaches thereof, for it hath been shaken.
59:5 Thou hast shown Thy people hard things, * Thou hast made us to drink the wine of contrition.
59:6 Thou hast given a sign unto them that fear Thee, * that they may flee from before the face of the bow.
59:7 That Thy beloved ones may be delivered, * save Thou with Thy right hand and hearken unto me.
59:8 God hath spoken in His sanctuary: * I will rejoice and I will divide Sikima, and the vale of tabernacles will I measure out.
59:9 Mine is Galaad, and Mine is Manasses, * and Ephraim is the strength of My head.
59:9 Judah is My king, * Moab is the cauldron of My hope.
59:10 Upon Idumea will I stretch out My shoe; * the foreign tribes have been subjected unto Me.
59:11 Who will bring me into a fortified city? * Or who will lead me into Idumea?
59:12 Wilt Thou not, O God, Who hast spurned us? * And wilt Thou not, O God, go forth with our forces?
59:13 Give us help from affliction, * for vain is the salvation of man.
59:14 In God we shall work mighty deeds, * and He will bring to nought them that afflict us.
