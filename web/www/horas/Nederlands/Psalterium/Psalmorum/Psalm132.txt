132:1 Behold how good and how pleasant it is * for brethren to dwell together in unity:
132:2a Like the precious ointment on the head, * that ran down upon the beard, the beard of Aaron,
132:2b Which ran down to the skirt of his garment: * (3a) as the dew of Hermon, which descendeth upon mount Sion.
132:3b For there the Lord hath commanded blessing, * and life for evermore.
