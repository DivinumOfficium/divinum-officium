Homily by St Jerome, Priest (at Bethlehem.)
!Book i. Comment, on Matth. ii.
He took the young Child, and His mother, and fled into Egypt, by night and in~
darkness; and the darkness of that night was a figure of the darkness of~
ignorance in which they left the unbelievers from whom they fled. But when they~
returned into Judaea, we learn not from the Gospel that it was by night, or in~
darkness; which is an image of that light which will lighten the Jews, when, at~
the end of the world, they shall receive back the faith, which now lighteneth~
the Gentiles, even as Judaea received Christ returning from Egypt.
