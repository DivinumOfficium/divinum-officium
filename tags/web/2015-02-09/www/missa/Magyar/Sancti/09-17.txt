[Oratio]
O Lord, Jesus Christ, Who, to inflame our hearts with the fire of Your love when~
the world was becoming cold in spirit, renewed in the flesh of most blessed~
Francis the sacred marks of Your own passion, graciously grant that by his~
merits and prayers we may steadfastly carry our cross and bring forth worthy~
fruits of penance.
