[Secreta]
May the humanity of Your only-begotten Son keep us, O Lord, for He, Who was born~
of a virgin did not lessen but consecrated the purity of His Mother; so, on the~
festival of her Visitation may Jesus Christ, our Lord, free us from our sins and~
make our sacrifice acceptable unto You.
