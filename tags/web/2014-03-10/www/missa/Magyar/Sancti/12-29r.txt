[Commemoratio Oratio]
!For S. Thomas Becket martyr
O God, for whose Church Bishop Thomas, now in glory, fell by the swords of~
wicked men, grant, we beseech You, that the prayers of all who implore his~
assistance may be effective and may lead to salvation.
$Per Dominum

[Commemoratio Secreta]
!For S. Thomas Becket martyr
O Lord, through the intercession of blessed Thomas, Your martyr and bishop,~
sanctify the offerings dedicated to You, and because of them, look upon us with~
mercy.
$Per Dominum

[Commemoratio Postcommunio]
!For S. Thomas Becket martyr
May this Communion, O Lord, cleanse us from sin and, by the intercession of~
blessed Thomas, Your martyr and bishop, impart to us heavenly healing.
$Per Dominum
