vide Sancti/08-10;
Gloria

[Commemoratio Oratio]
!Commemoratio Ss. Mart. Hippolyti et Cassiani.
Grant, we beseech You, almighty God, that the great festival of Your blessed~
Martyrs, Hippolytus and Cassian, may further both our holiness and our~
salvation.
$Per Dominum

[Commemoratio Secreta]
!Commemoratio Ss. Mart. Hippolyti et Cassiani.
Look with favor, O Lord, upon the offerings of Your people on the devout~
festival of these Saints, and may their witness to Your truth profit for our~
salvation.
$Per Dominum

[Commemoratio Postcommunio]
!Commemoratio Ss. Mart. Hippolyti et Cassiani.
May the eating of Your sacrament save us, O Lord, and make us stand firm in the~
light of Your truth.
$Per Dominum
