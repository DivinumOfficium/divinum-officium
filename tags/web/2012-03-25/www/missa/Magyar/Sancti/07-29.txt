[Commemoratio Oratio]
!Ss Felicis, Simplicii, Faustini et Beatricis.
Grant, we beseech You, O Lord, that as Christian people on earth rejoice~
together on the feast of Your Martyrs, Felix, Simplicius, Faustinus and Beatrice,~
so may they enjoy it in eternity, and may truly obtain what they honor in their~
prayers.
