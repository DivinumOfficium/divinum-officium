vide C2a;

[Oratio]
O God, You Who give us the joy of celebrating the anniversary of the death of~
blessed Saturninus, Your Martyr, grant that we may be helped by his merits.
$Per Dominum

[Secreta]
O Lord, make holy the sacrificial gifts we offer to You, and through the~
intercession of blessed Saturninus, Your Martyr, be appeased and look with favor~
upon us.
$Per Dominum

[Postcommunio]
May the reception of Your sacrament, we beseech You, O Lord, make us holy, and~
by the intercession of Your saints, make us pleasing to You.
$Per Dominum
