[Oratio]
Look forgivingly on thy flock, Eternal Shepherd, and keep it in thy constant protection, by the intercession of blessed Cletus and Marcellinus, martyrs and Sovereign Pontiffs, whom thou didst constitute Shepherd of the whole Church.
