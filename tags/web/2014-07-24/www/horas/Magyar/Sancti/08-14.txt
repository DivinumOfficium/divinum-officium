[Commemoratio]
!Commemoration of S. Eusebii.
@Commune/C4:Oratio proper
$Oremus
O God, Who, year by year, dost gladden us by the solemn Feast-day of Thy blessed Confessor, Eusebius, mercifully grant unto all who keep his birthday, grace to follow after the pattern of his godly conversation.
$Per Dominum

[Oratio]
O God, Who wast pleased to choose for thy dwelling-place the maiden palace of Blessed Mary, grant, we beseech thee, that her protection may shield us, and make us glad in her commemoration.
$Qui vivis
