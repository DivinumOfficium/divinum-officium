[Commemoratio Oratio]
!For Four Crowned Martyrs
Grant, we beseech You, almighty God, that we who know how courageously Your holy~
Martyrs have confessed the faith, may experience their goodness as they~
intercede for us with You.
$Per Dominum

[Commemoratio Secreta]
!For Four Crowned Martyrs
May Your bountiful blessing descend upon us, O Lord, and by the prayers of Your~
holy Martyrs may it make our gifts acceptable to You, and make them a sacrament~
of redemption for us.
$Per Dominum

[Commemoratio Postcommunio]
!For Four Crowned Martyrs
We who have been fed with heavenly sacraments and joys, humbly pray You, O Lord,~
that we may enjoy the protection of those in honor of whose victories we~
rejoice.
$Per Dominum
